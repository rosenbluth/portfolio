UX

I have a good sense of empathy, along with an ability to effectively evaluate what the user’s current needs are, and what the future needs may be. My mix of skills allow me to be a good communicator to the technical side of a team, as well as that stakeholders, and the design team.

Front End Developer

After completing the Galvanize six month full stack web development immersive program, my technical skills are sharp. The program gave me hands on experience with agile methodology, numerous JavaScript frameworks, writing user stories and identifying specific problems. Building out websites from conception through completion, including the conceptual sketches, wireframes, turing mockups into functional websites, JavaScript, styling, and markup languages were also a components of my education. I always continue to learn and improve my skill set. I am currently focused on ReactJS.
