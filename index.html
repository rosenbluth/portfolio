Skills



UX

An effective UX designer must imaginatively and creatively solve problems and keep up with the fast pace of technology; these skills require that a UX designer have a well-developed sense of empathy and also continue learning about their craft in order to bring fresh knowledge to a project. Ongoing education is a priority for me.  I continue to learn, whether in a class or from current instructional books. I have a well-developed sense of empathy which gives me the ability to evaluate a user’s present needs and to anticipate what future needs may be.  Information Architecture is a specialty. As a team player, I take feedback well and I am able to make important changes during the iteration process.  Flexibility is key to excellent design.  My mix of design and technical skills allows me to communicate well with stakeholders, design teams, and the technical members of a team.  Additionally, deciphering user flow problems and determining feasible solutions are two strong abilities.   Past employment has provided me with real world experience speaking with clients to find out their needs and to design efficient solutions to business problems.



Front End Developer

After completing the Galvanize six month full stack web development immersive program, my technical skills are sharp. The program provided hands-on experience with agile methodology, numerous JavaScript frameworks, writing user stories and identifying specific problems. I always continue to learn and improve my skill set. I am currently focused on ReactJS and JSX.

Fighter Tracker

Using an Ajax call to the UFC api

The UFC, and MMA in general, is overlooked by the mainstream sports media. Although this has become less of a problem in the past few years, it is still hard to find reliable information. This project is a way to add your fighters and track them. I still need to add a bunch of features, such as: an alert that pops up when a UFC event is coming up, a table row that adds when a fighters next fight will be, a section that gives news updates, and a few more things. I learned a lot during this project. I had some road blocks along the way but I feel that this project greatly improved my skills. I would have done a better job planning out my project in the beginning. Time management was an issue. It was hard to estimate how long tasks would take. I would have made my trello board differently. I would like to add more features. GitHub Link Here
